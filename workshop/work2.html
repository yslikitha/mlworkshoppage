INFORMATION

Photography is an online competition, which encourages the participants to showcase their photographic skills that help them capture cherishable moments. Megalith through this competition tries to uplift the creative and imaginative skills of participants.

PROBLEM STATEMENT


Photography takes an instant out of time, altering life by holding it still.Megalith, IIT Kharagpur presents a unique photography event where participants are required to submit a photograph of any ongoing or completed construction of a project in their localities.

Note:

1. Send your photograph and write-up to photography@megalith.co.in, along with your full name, enrolled college name, mobile number and email ID.

2. Submissions should include a short write up(not more than 200 words) describing the activity that is happening on the site at the time the photograph was taken.

3. Deadline for Photography Submission is 22nd March 2022 before 11:59 pm.


RULES


❖ EligibilityThe competition is open to all students enrolled in any Indian college.

❖Images that have won prizes in prior competitions, or have been used for commercial purposes and/or been published will not be eligible.

❖ Submission of Entries should be in JPEG format and at least 1600 pixels wide for a horizontal image or 1600 pixels tall for a vertical image.

❖ Each participant can submit one (1) entry only.

❖ Digital manipulation that distorts the reality of the photos will not be allowed. Only basic enhancements such as sharpening, exposure, colour and contrast adjustment, or simple cropping will be allowed.

❖ The organiser reserves the right to reject images with incomplete details required under the photo submission page.

❖ The file name for each photograph should be clearly labelled with the participant's name. The email should contain the participant's name, phone no. & enrolled college name.

❖ The Organisers reserve the right to reject entries with incomplete details.

❖ Participants are entirely responsible for all entry-related costs.

❖ Judging and Results The images will be posted on the Megalith Facebook page and 30% weightage will be given to the sum of likes and shares on the image. Points awarded by a panel of judges will carry 40% weightage. The quality of write-up bears the rest 30%. No correspondence pertaining to the selection process and decision will be entertained.

❖ The panel of judges will evaluate the entries based on the artistic/visual appeal and the extent to which the photograph captures the essence of the theme.

❖ The judging panel reserves the right not to award the prizes if the photographs submitted do not meet the requirements stated in this document.

❖Results of the competition will be declared during the closing ceremony.

❖The winning photographs will feature on the official Megalith website and the most popular entries will be showcased in a web gallery on the website with due acknowledgements.
